Why should we track calories?

  1. Your body counts them, so you need to as well
  2. People suck at estimating calorie intake and expenditure
  3. It makes monitoring progress easier
  4. People who tell you calories don't count are usually promoting a guru system or a dogmatic approach
  5. It allows for diary freedom
